The Retreat

Nestled in a peaceful part of Marloth Park and overlooking beautiful parkland, Minia's Kruger Retreat offers a stylish and comfortable bushveld escape, ideal for families or groups.

This well-designed home accommodates up to 6 adults and 2 children, with all three bedrooms located downstairs and featuring en-suite bathrooms, air-conditioning, ceiling fans, and sliding doors that open onto private patio areas.

The open-plan living space is light and inviting, with a fully equipped kitchen, dining area, and lounge fitted with air-conditioning, Wi-Fi, and streaming access for relaxed indoor living.

The outdoor area captures the true essence of bushveld living, with a covered patio, built-in braai, and a traditional boma/fire pit for evenings under the stars. A private swimming pool provides the perfect place to cool off on warm Lowveld days, while the parkland views allow guests to enjoy regular wildlife sightings right from the comfort of the home. The property also offers added peace of mind with an alarm system and the convenience of a generator to assist during power interruptions.
